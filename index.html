Popcorn Popper


Can you become a popcorn sensation?


Click on a fuse piece to rotate it clockwise. Fuse pieces can be rotated any number of times.


A red fuse links to a flame; a green fuse links to a kernel.


Rotate pieces to create a continuous fuse path between a flame and a kernel to pop a piece of popcorn!


But hurry- you must pop enough for this popcorn-loving kitty before the movie starts or the show will not go on...
